Welcome to The Pepper Plate

The Pepper Plate is a cloud Kitchen concept arising out of our passion for cooking. We intend to be a conscious driven Kitchen wherein we take exceptional measures to make sure that what you get is best quality ingredient with mouthwatering taste. The Pepper Plate is family centric business venture wherein we endeavor to bring to you what we have being cooking at our homes from time immemorial. The menu encapsulates the dishes which we have mastered over the years and have received fabulous reviews for the same. We are foodies who equally enjoy eating as much as serving them to other people. The idea is to bring the magical flavors of our kitchen to your table. We make sure that what you get is the best quality and fresh ingredients, flavor, best level of hygiene and cleanliness standards.
